📖 How to Install
1
Enter your clinic slug in the configurator. Your slug is the part after ?clinic= in your booking URL.
2
Choose your widget type — Floating button is most popular. Works on any website without affecting the design.
3
Copy the code and paste it into your website. If you have a web developer, send them the code.
4
Done! The booking button appears on your site. It connects to Dentoq — so even if your website goes down, patients can still book.
